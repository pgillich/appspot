Museums

Nowadays I've seen more technical museums.

Hungarian Museum of Transport, Budapest
The makings of this museum are good, but - unfortunately - not utilized. For example, the museum is not at all interactive. Let's see the marine navigation lights. More different navigation lights are exhibited, but there aren't any description or help what the matter are these subjects there. Why are exhibited? How it work? A simple computer simulation or a toy-like installation could present the essence of the marine navigation. It could be combined with the exhibited radars. It's only one example...
Exhibition of Road Collection, Kiskőrös
I've been to there more times in my childhood. Since this time, the museum has been reconstructed. It has been become interactive (including the cafe shop, too). It seems a lot of money has spent for the reconstruction, but unfortunately no money for looking after the exhibited subjects.
