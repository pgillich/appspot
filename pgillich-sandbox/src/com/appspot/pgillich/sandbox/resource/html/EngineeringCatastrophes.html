Fukushima

When I first heard in the news that we didn't have to afraid of missing cooling of the reactors, because an other reactor was hardly melted in same situation (Three Mile Island), I got panic.
At the three-mile-island disaster, a valve status was confused. After setting it manually, the cooling was solved. But the cooling system was totally destroyed at Fukushima!
